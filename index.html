About Me

I’m Nomikos Skyllas, a climate and ecological data scientist with a background in geospatial analysis, remote sensing, and environmental modeling. My work focuses on how climate change is reshaping Arctic ecosystems, particularly through its effects on migratory birds and seasonal dynamics. I enjoy bridging the gap between ecological insight and data-driven methods — especially when it leads to practical or policy-relevant insights.
