# Nomikos Skyllas
Climate & Ecological Modelling

## Introduction
I work at the intersection of climate science and ecology, focusing on how environmental change shapes animal systems. My work combines climate data, ecological knowledge, and modelling to build clear and defensible analyses.

I am particularly interested in migratory systems, where large-scale atmospheric processes interact with behavioural and ecological constraints.

## What I do
- Develop data-driven models linking climate and ecology  
- Analyse large environmental datasets (climate, satellite, tracking data)  
- Translate ecological questions into quantitative frameworks  
- Evaluate assumptions, uncertainty, and model behaviour
