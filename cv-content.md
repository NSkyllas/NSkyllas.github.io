## Experience

### PhD Researcher
University of Groningen / KNMI / IBED  
2022 – 2026  

Research focused on how climate change affects Arctic migratory birds, combining climate models, satellite data, and tracking data.

Key work:
- Developed a mechanistic model to simulate migratory flyways based on environmental constraints  
- Analysed shifts in snowmelt, vegetation timing, and reproductive windows  
- Integrated climate model output (CMIP6) with ecological datasets  
- Worked across disciplines: climate science, ecology, and movement modelling  

### Collaboration & projects
Worked closely with ecologists and climate scientists on interdisciplinary projects, including:
- Vulnerability of Arctic migratory birds to rapid climate change  
- Linking environmental change to migration behaviour and breeding success
